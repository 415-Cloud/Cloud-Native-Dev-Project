## Logging Workouts
Keep track of your physical activities in the **Workout Log**.

<img width="1218" height="872" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-16 at 8 27 17‚ÄØPM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ed8847cd-213f-4d24-8570-93c01469154a" />

1.  Click **"Workout Log"** in the navigation bar.
2.  **Fill out the form:**
    *   **Activity Type:** Select from Running, Cycling, Walking, Swimming, etc.
    *   **Date:** Choose the date of the workout (defaults to today).
    *   **Duration:** Enter the time spent in minutes.
    *   **Distance:** (Optional) Enter distance in miles.
    *   **Calories Burned:** (Optional) Enter estimated calories burned.
    *   **Notes:** Add any specific details about the session.
3.  Click **"Log Workout"**.
4.  **History:** Your recent workouts are listed below the form. You can **Delete** a workout if you made a mistake.

## Challenges
Compete with others or challenge yourself!

<img width="1219" height="868" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-16 at 8 27 06‚ÄØPM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ad0ab6c1-9ba0-4bb0-b7f6-e28b1c06827d" />


### Joining a Challenge
1.  Click **"Challenges"** in the navigation bar.
2.  Browse the list of available active challenges.
    *   Challenges show their difficulty, target goal, and end date.
3.  Click **"Join Challenge"** on any card to participate.
4.  Your progress in the challenge will be automatically tracked based on your logged workouts.

### Creating a Challenge
Want to start your own competition?
![add image here]

1.  On the Challenges screen, click the **"Create Challenge"** button in the top right.
2.  **Fill in the details:**
    *   **Name:** Give it a catchy title.
    *   **Description:** Explain the rules or goal.
    *   **Type:** Choose the metric (Distance, Workout Count, or Calories).
    *   **Target Value:** The goal to reach (e.g., 50 miles).
    *   **Dates:** Set the Start and End dates.
3.  Click **"Create Challenge"**. It will now be visible to all users.

### Viewing Details
Click **"View Details"** on any challenge card (from the Dashboard or Challenges list) to see:
*   Full description and stats.
*   **Participant List:** See who else has joined and their current progress percentage.



### Leaving & Deleting
*   **Leave:** If you joined a challenge but want to quit, click **"Leave Challenge"**.
*   **Delete:** If **YOU** created the challenge, you will see a red **"Delete"** button. This removes the challenge for everyone. **Warning: This cannot be undone.**

## AI Training Plan
Get a personalized weekly schedule generated by AI.

<img width="1211" height="869" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-16 at 8 27 59‚ÄØPM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7b0ba2d1-88d1-43c2-ba52-e981d851d797" />


1.  Click **"Training Plan"** in the navigation bar.
2.  The AI analyzes your profile (Age, Goals, Fitness Level) to build a custom plan.
3.  **View Plan:** You will see a list of recommended exercises for upcoming days.
4.  **AI Advice:** Read personalized tips and motivation from your AI Coach.
5.  **Persistence:** Your plan is saved automatically. It won't disappear when you leave the page.
6.  **Regenerate:** If you want a fresh plan, click the **"Generate New Training Plan"** button in the top right.
